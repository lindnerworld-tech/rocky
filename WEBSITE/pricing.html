Billing details

Rocky Plus is an automatically renewing subscription. The monthly plan renews each month and the annual plan renews each year unless canceled. Applicable tax is calculated during checkout. Stripe Managed Payments serves as merchant of record, securely processes payment information, and may display the purchase as sold through Link.

Cancellation and refunds

You may manage or cancel your subscription through Stripe's secure customer portal or by contacting Project Rocky support. Cancellation stops future renewals. Refund requests made within 14 days of a charge are eligible under our Refund Policy.
