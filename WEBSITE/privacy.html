Privacy Policy

What Project Rocky processes, why we use it, and the choices available to you.

Effective date: July 17, 2026

1. Who we are

Project Rocky is operated by Waikahe Orchards LLC, a Hawaii limited liability company ("Waikahe Orchards," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use rockyaloha.com and related Project Rocky services (the "Service").

2. Information we process

Account information

When you create or use an account, our authentication provider processes identifiers and sign-in information such as your email address, account ID, verification status, and session information.

Rocky questions and responses

We process the category and question you submit so the Service can generate a response. We do not intentionally store the raw text of your Rocky questions in our own account database. The input and generated response are processed by our AI and infrastructure providers to deliver and protect the Service.

Usage and security information

We process information such as daily usage counts, plan and entitlement status, approximate request time, IP address or network-derived security signals, device or browser information, and anti-abuse verification results. This helps enforce allowances, prevent fraud and automated abuse, diagnose errors, and keep the Service reliable.

Subscription and transaction information

If you purchase Rocky Plus, we receive subscription and transaction identifiers, product or price information, subscription status, billing-period dates, and a customer identifier from Paddle. Paddle processes your payment details as merchant of record. We do not receive or store your full payment-card number.

Communications

If you contact us, we process your email address and the information in your message so we can respond and keep an appropriate support record.

3. How we use information

  • Provide Rocky responses, accounts, subscription access, and daily allowances.
  • Authenticate users and protect accounts.
  • Process purchases, cancellations, entitlement updates, and refund support.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, security incidents, and service disruption.
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Service.
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

4. Service providers

We use service providers to operate Rocky. These currently include Cloudflare for hosting, security, and anti-abuse services; Clerk for account authentication; OpenAI for AI processing; and Paddle for checkout, subscription billing, tax handling as merchant of record, and buyer support. These providers process information under their own terms and privacy commitments and only for the functions they provide to us.

5. Cookies and local storage

The Service and its providers may use cookies, browser storage, and similar technologies that are necessary for sign-in, security, abuse prevention, session continuity, and basic site operation. We do not use Rocky account information to sell targeted advertising.

6. How we share information

We share information with the service providers described above as needed to operate the Service. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights or safety, to investigate fraud or misuse, or as part of a business reorganization subject to appropriate safeguards. We do not sell personal information for money and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

7. Retention

We keep account, entitlement, usage, transaction-reference, security, and support information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described here, including providing the Service, maintaining financial and security records, resolving disputes, and meeting legal obligations. Retention periods vary by data type and provider. We may retain de-identified or aggregated information that no longer identifies you.

8. Your choices and privacy rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we control by emailing support@rockyaloha.com. Depending on where you live, you may also have rights to receive a copy, restrict or object to processing, or appeal a privacy-request decision. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. You may manage subscription cancellation separately using the subscription-management link provided with your purchase.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so please use care when choosing what to submit.

10. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

11. International processing

We and our providers may process information in the United States and other countries. Privacy laws may differ from those where you live. Where required, providers use recognized safeguards for international transfers.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service changes. We will post the revised policy with a new effective date and provide additional notice when required by law.
