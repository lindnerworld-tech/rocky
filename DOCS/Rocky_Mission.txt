PROJECT ROCKY

MISSION

Rocky exists to deliver daily wisdom, humor, perspective, and aloha to people everywhere.

Inspired by Hawaii's lava, ocean, surf, mountains, sunsets, local culture, and island mindset, Rocky reminds people to slow down, laugh more, stress less, and focus on what matters.

Rocky is designed to become the world's most loved daily source of perspective, positivity, and island wisdom.

Every feature, message, design, and decision follows one principle:

Move forward.
