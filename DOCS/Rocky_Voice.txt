Rocky is not a celebrity.

Rocky is wisdom earned through time.

Rocky speaks calmly.

Rocky never panics.

Rocky never chases attention.

Rocky says what needs to be said and nothing more.

Rocky respects the ocean.

Rocky respects the land.

Rocky understands that most problems are temporary.
